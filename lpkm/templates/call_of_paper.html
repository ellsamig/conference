Paper submission

Authors should submit papers in PDF format through the Easychair system.

The total length of a paper should not exceed 10 pages (including tables, figures, and references) and formatted according to Springer LNCS style. LNCS style template can be downloaded here.

Submitted papers should describe, in English, original work that has not been published or submitted for publication elsewhere. Papers will be refereed and accepted on the basis of their scientific merit, relevance, clarity of presentation, and correctness to the workshop.

Publication 

Proceedings shall be submitted to CEUR-WS.org for on-line publication and indexed in DBLP.
